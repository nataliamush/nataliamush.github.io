    Natalia Mushegian

Hi, I'm Natalia!

There’s nothing I’m more motivated by and invested in than slowing climate change - I’m interested in regenerative agriculture and forest management, and would love to help farmers and landowners acquire the financial resources and knowledge to implement carbon-negative practices on their land.

I’m a quantitative thinker with a love of working in complex systems, as well as communicating complexity to a variety of audiences. I’m interested in being involved in organizations that help bring about systematic change through policy or technology, or ones that instill cultural shifts through education, outreach, and extension!

In my spare time, I swing dance, hike, ferment foods, and eat vegetables. I’d like to cultivate my gardening hobby (to farm both food and carbon), as well as my propensity for building things, as soon as I settle in somewhere with access to a patch of soil and power tools!

Check out my experience page to hear about how I’ve kept myself busy over the past few years, and some reflection on what I’ve learned. My work page includes projects, papers, posters, and more to give you an idea of what I’ve produced! And feel free to reach out to me via one of my social links or the form on my contact page!
